Eminent Clarion Group Limited

CORPORATE RECRUITMENT SERVICE

Corporate Recruitment is about employing to corporate institutions like government agencies, limited liability companies, small scale enterprises and many more. These employees are being taken through interview training, customer care and communications training, work ethics and various workshops before being placed to various agencies and companies to work.

DOMESTIC RECRUITMENT SERVICE

This service is about supplying special domestic workers such as house help, Nanny, cooks, cleaners, home care nurses, care takers, gardeners and etc to our various clients. These domestic workers will be trained on fire fighting and first Aid services so they can be of help at their various places of work in case of emergency. these domestic workers will also be in uniforms and properly dressed.

CORPORATE CHAUFFER SERVICES

CORPORATE CHAUFFEUR (DRIVING) SERVICES: this services is about providing qualified and experienced drivers to our clients based on their specifications. This special drivers are trained on first Aid service, fire fighting and defensive driving. This special skills will help them provide emergency assistance also aside driving. this drivers will have company ID cards and labeled reflectors.

COMMERCIAL ACCOUNTING SERVICES

This service is about preparing basic accounts to SMEs and also giving them financial counseling. Some of the accounts has to do with Payroll, Profit and loose, Tax returns,Balance sheet and many more.

FOREIGN RECRUITMENT SERVICES

FOREIGN RECRUITMENT SERVICES (Travel & Tour ) This service is about recruiting people to work outside Ghana or work in foreign countries. The service also includes passport acquisition, birth certificate acquisition, Hotel booking, Fun trip organizing and any other related activities.
